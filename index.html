Welcome to the SGFwiki!

SGFwiki is a free & socialist wiki aiming to bring factual information from a leftist perspective without the negative rightist influence. Content will range from politics to history to economics. For now, SGFwiki is still a work-in-progress and will be expanded overtime. If you would like to contribute some material, please contact us.

Contact Us

If you would like to get in touch, you can reach us at support@sgf.wiki with a small chance of getting a response. If you would like a certain chance of getting a response, simply contact us via our Discord.
