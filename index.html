About SGFwiki.

SGF is an organization based on the idea of spreading the teachings of Marx and Lenin to those who seek it, bringing growth to the global class consciousness and ultimately pushing the undying goal of socialism. Founded on the principle of sharing knowledge, SGF has witnessed significant growth, and thus, the amount of importance placed on this information's access has increased. SGF, through this growth, will never fall back on it's original goals, and will hopefully only expand from here.

If you wish to contribute to the SGFwiki, please contact us via email or in the Discord server.

Things to note:

  • Any contributions are reviewed before deciding whether to add it to the site or not. It is possible your submission may be edited or revised.
  • Your contribution will be automatically denied if it is not relevant to the many aspects of society, including: history, politics, economics, etc.
  • You will be contacted with an update on whether your contribution was accepted or denied.
  • It is possible that your contribution gets approved after already having been denied.
